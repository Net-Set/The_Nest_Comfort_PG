                                                  THE NEST COMFORT PG IS MANAGED BY MOUNT RENTERS AND FURNISHERS

 

With the owners being based on site, any problems or issues are dealt with effectively and without time delays if and when they arise.With the addition of our security guards being on site, there is always help available if needed.

 

Our Housekeeping team is on site 7 days per week to ensure that all communal areas and shared facilities are cleaned on a regular basis.Cooking is handled by professional cooks completely under the supervision of the owners, making sure that the food quality is hygienic and matches the finger licking taste of your mother’s kitchen.

 

 Doctor,beautician and tailor services are provided on request with the permission of the authority.

 

PRIVACY POLICY

 

All the information provided by you,would be used in accordance to respective laws.

REFUND POLICY

Any booking which is done through our website is non refundable.

Only the deposit is Refundable for inmates already staying at the property with a written notice of 30 days prior to your leaving the PG

 

Terms and Conditions

All Terms and Conditions for staying in our PG are mentioned in Rules 

At The Nest Comfort PG we pride ourselves on the relationship we build with our students.As the owners are based solely at The Nest Comfort PG, this gives us the opportunity to interact with and get to know each of our students.

 

We believe the relationships we build make for an enjoyable and memorable stay here and students are more likely to approach us for any problems they may have or just for a general chat. As we get to know our students well, we can offer help or direct them to appropriate sources if we recognize that they are having problems.

 

The safety of our students is always our first and foremost concern. It is for this reason that we have 24x7 Security Guards. We also have CCTV cameras installed in all major areas and a BIometric Attendance System.

 

The presence of the Owners and Security Guards ensures that the safety and peace of mind of all of our students is secure. As well as preventing any unwanted visitors and safeguarding the building.

You can use the contact form  to ask us any questions you may have, or to arrange a viewing of the property. Our office is open from 9am to 5pm Monday to Saturday. If you require a viewing outside of these hours, just inform us of your preferred day/time and we will  arrange for the same.
