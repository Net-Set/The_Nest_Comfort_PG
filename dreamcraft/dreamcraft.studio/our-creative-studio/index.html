Our Designing Process

When making a house there are many things to consider and the client wants to get it right. So we follow a process that covers all the essential factors in designing of the house along the way.

1st Step

Measurement / Drawing

Each step involves many factors to consider. The first step incorporates the client's requirement, measurement, sketch, and design development. The science of building technical information and aesthetic designs are all considered.

2st Step

Zoning

While starting the process of zoning, we try to know what your lifestyle is and what has to happen in each space. We come up with a number of designs and allow our clients to prefer the best design as it takes a bit of thought and patience.

3st Step

Discussion

To bring a carefully edited selection of the best architecture design and interiors, we take the valuable thoughts of clients into consideration and discuss with them to finalize the design.

4st Step

Elevation Development

We believe that every interior should reflect the client's ideas images and goals. For that, we work with dedication and take into consideration each and every detail of a project thus achieving the ultimate contemporary style with special focus on decoration, space usage, furniture, interior lighting, etc.

5st Step

3D Modeling

We also provide our clients to get a clear vision of the project through the 3D model as it allows us to present the beauty and functionality of works in the best life.

Our Creative Team

Jay Gajjar

"One must take vacations to rekindle life into themselves and their design"

A passionate designer believing in phenomenal idealism and a pragmatic problem solver. I am a graduate in architecture and behold a master's degree in management. My hobbies include nature photography and traveling. Inspired by Frank Gehry "Architecture should speak of its time and place but yearn for timelessness." Being Indian I firmly believe in taking our traditional roots of VASTUSHASTRA ahead. Vastushastra enhances the energy in the space for a peaceful and prosperous pattern of life. The effortless amalgamation of Vastu in contemporary architecture and lifestyle is the key philosophy of my design.

Kandarp Jani

An architect by profession and a sedulous learner by choice. My interests involve exploring the roots of design from their philosophies to their form. A reader, who likes creating his own library. One of my favorites from the collection is ‘Inside Out’ by Architect Peter Eisenman which defines different visions by various architects on how to connect interiors with the exteriors. Philosophy: I believe: the true essence of a designer lies within his way of connecting time, context, and character in architecture. I believe there should be a significant correspondence between the interiors and exteriors. What defines the fineness of design are its details and simplicity. ”Details refine architecture”. The design should be timeless, explore forms, reflect minimalism & should feature details.” With the combined knowledge of each factor, you can create wonder.”
